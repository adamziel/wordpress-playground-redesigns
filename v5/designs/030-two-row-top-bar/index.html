v5Slot 030Top-bar refinement of #64 DevTools

Two-row top bar

Row 1 is who you are — the URL, the saved name, the brand. It never changes.
Row 2 is what you can do — three modes (Site · Manage · Library). The verbs in row 2 swap to match the active mode. One secondary surface (a docked panel under row 2) opens when a verb needs a sustained workspace.

STATE A — Working session, default Site mode. No dock. Iframe is the document. 8affordances visible at idle

The default. Row 1 carries identity (brand · refresh · URL · saved-name + pill · more · fullscreen). Row 2 is in Site mode: three mode tabs on the left, three contextual verbs (Sites, Settings, Save) on the right. Click Manage or Library and row 2's verbs change. The iframe gets 760 of 800 vertical pixels.
