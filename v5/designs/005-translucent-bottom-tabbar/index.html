v5 / 005 — Translucent bottom tab bar

Mobile-first refinement of #06. A single frosted tab bar floats over the WordPress iframe; tapping a tab raises one bottom sheet — Sites (launcher), Manage (Site Manager), or Menu (overflow). The bar fades to 0.15 when the iframe scrolls and returns on touch, so the WordPress site is the loudest thing in every state.
