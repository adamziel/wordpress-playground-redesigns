One bottom sheet for every panel — Settings, Files, Blueprint, Database, Logs, Sites. Drag a single handle to one of three detents: peek (40px status, iframe owns the screen), half (45%, working with the iframe), or full (cinematic gallery / fullscreen tool). Same gesture, same surface, on phone and desktop. The iframe is always the document.
