How the IA collapses

Old chrome: top toolbar (7 affordances) + left Site Manager sidebar (5 tabs + 3 header buttons) + right Saved-Playgrounds drawer (3 stacked sections + 43-blueprint sub-screen) + popovers. New chrome: a 44px top bar (6 chips) + a 24px pull-tab. The tab opens one pane at 25/50/75% with six modes: Sites · Settings · Files · Blueprint · Database · Logs. The triple settings doors are gone; the launcher is the Sites mode; the drawer is gone. Apply & Reset takes a typed phrase. Press F to fullscreen the iframe; Esc exits. Press ⌘\ to toggle the pane. Same affordances at 1440 and 390 — desktop is a wider phone.
