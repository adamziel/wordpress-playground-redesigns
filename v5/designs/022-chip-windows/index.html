Chip windows

WordPress Playground · v5 #022. The five Site Manager sections live in the top toolbar as small window-chips — each is a minimized macOS-style window. Click a chip to materialize that section as a small floating, draggable card over the iframe. The chip animates into the card and back. Multiple cards are allowed but rare — the design pushes you toward one card at a time, the way a tab bar pushes you toward one tab.

F Fullscreen iframe · Esc exit / dismiss top card · ⌘1–⌘5 toggle a chip · ⌘K command palette
