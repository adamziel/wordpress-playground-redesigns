Quiet-rail-to-loud — WordPress Playground
A 56-pixel monochrome rail on the left is the entire idle chrome. Tap an icon and a single 320-pixel pane shouts beside it — Sites, Site Manager, or Blueprints. The iframe shrinks but never disappears. Tap the X (or the icon again) and the pane retracts; the rail quiets back down. One secondary surface, three doors, three states, no duplicates. Esc collapses everything to fullscreen.

#018 · Quiet-rail-to-loud · WordPress Playground v5

Direction parent: v1 #55 (iPad multitasking) + v1 #14 (Ventura Settings). Refines the "one pane, swaps content" principle by reducing the persistent rail to 56 pixels of monochrome restraint, and the secondary surface to a single 320-pixel pane that always appears in the same place. Every chrome decision aims to make the iframe and the rail the only two things you ever look at.
