Iframe Supreme
One law: the WordPress iframe never shrinks below 65% of the viewport — not when settings are open, not when files are open, not when 43 blueprints are being browsed. The chrome is a 44 px glass top bar, a single floating dock, and one swappable bottom sheet capped at 30% of screen height (so iframe + topbar ≥ 70%). Desktop is just a wider version of the same composition.
