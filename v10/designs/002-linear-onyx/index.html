Linear Onyx · token reference. Matte near-black surface, six-step gray ramp from #0A0A0B to #E6E6E8, single indigo→fuchsia accent. Borders are 1px hairlines at rgba(255,255,255,0.06) or 0.5px subpixel separators at rgba(255,255,255,0.04). Radii: 10px outer, 6–8px inner, 999px for pills.
