What collapsed

Three settings entry points become one Settings sheet (reachable from the toolbar slider, the More tab, and the Unsaved notice — same surface every time). The Site Manager sidebar dissolves into the bottom tabs.

One-thumb rules

Every primary action is within thumb-reach of the bottom edge: tabs, sheet handles, sheet footers, the toast's Undo. The destructive "Apply & Reset" is intentionally moved to the top of a confirm sheet, never auto-triggerable.

Why desktop still works

Desktop reuses the same column — no separate layout to maintain — and unhides side rails for power users who want files + logs visible at once. New users see the same UI on both devices.
