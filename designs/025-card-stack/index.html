The pitch

Playground as a dating app. The 43 blueprints become a swipeable deck — one card, one decision. Swipe right to launch; left to skip; up to peek the blueprint JSON and target versions; down to bookmark for later. Your saved sites are Matches; Site Manager dissolves into per-match drawers; the settings popover becomes your profile preferences. Three settings entry points collapse into one. The full chrome (toolbar, drawer, sidebar, popovers, tabs) reduces to a five-tab dating-app frame that feels identical on mobile and desktop.
