Recipe №02 · Coffee Shop
Chef's Special
https://playground.wordpress.net/scope:0d8e/2025/03/coffee-shop-launch/
Espresso Bar + New Howdy, admin
Single-origin · Fair trade · Slow brewed

The first sip
tells a story.

Hand-roasted in small batches, our beans travel from Yirgacheffe to your cup in under fourteen days. Order online or visit the bar.

Shop now Find a café

Ingredients

  • 1 themeWabi — a clean WooCommerce storefront
  • 2 pluginsWooCommerce 9.4, Stripe Gateway 8.7
  • a pinchSample products (24), reviews (12), customers (8)
  • to tasteCustom block patterns & navigation menus
  • opt.One spoonful of dummy WooPayments data

Method

  1. Pre-heat WordPress 6.7.2 with PHP 8.2 until softly bubbling.
  2. Sift in the Wabi theme and activate.
  3. Fold in WooCommerce; wait for it to bloom (~3s).
  4. Stir in the sample product set with featured images.
  5. Garnish with a homepage pattern and primary nav. Serve warm.
