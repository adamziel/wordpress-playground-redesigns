WordPress Playground — iPad multitasking

The WordPress iframe is the primary app. A secondary pane lives in Split View beside it — swap it (Files, Blueprint, Logs, DB, Settings) via the iPadOS three-dot pull-tab. The launcher is a Slide Over card. Saved sites live in a Dock at the bottom edge. Drag the divider to bias either pane; pull all the way over to dismiss Split View.
