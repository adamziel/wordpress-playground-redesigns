Design#086·Top + bottom toolbar (no sidebars)

Twin rails. No sidebars. Iframe-first.

The chrome is two horizontal bands. The top rail never changes (identity, URL, save, launcher, settings). The bottom dock is context-aware: pick a mode on the left, the rest of the dock fills with the verbs you need. Heavy surfaces rise as bottom sheets, leaving the WordPress iframe visible above.
