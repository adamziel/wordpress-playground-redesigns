v7 / 004 Smart resize · Sites corner SR + CWS

Smart-resize is the layout (two panes that re-weight to what you touch). The single piece of persistent chrome above the iframe is a frosted Sites widget in the top-left corner that shows live status — site name + state — and is also the canonical door to the launcher. Click it, and the secondary pane swaps into Sites mode and the gauge snaps to 40 / 60 in the pane's favor. Everything else (Settings, Files, Blueprint, DB, Logs) lives behind quiet tabs at the pane's head. One door per feature, one secondary surface, one bar, one widget.
