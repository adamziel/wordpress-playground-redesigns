Primitive composition · what's reused

  • SR (Smart-resize, v5 #023): Two panes, focus-driven snap at 35/65, 50/50, 60/40, 75/25; F for 100/0 fullscreen, Esc returns. Momentum gauge is etched in the seam (passive, never draggable).
  • EPC (Epic launcher, v1 #13 + v5 #044): The cinematic hero IS the right pane when no site is running. One decisive verb: LAUNCH. When boot begins, the launcher visibly recedes (Frame B: blur + opacity .5 + scale .98) and the pane snaps to 25%.
  • DEV (v1 #64): 44 px slim browser-style top bar; the save-state pill (Unsaved / Saving / Saved / Booting) lives where HTTPS lives. No two-row chrome.
  • 3LD visual (v5 #035): Used only as the recede transition — blur 0→2 px on the receding launcher pane in Frames B/D/G when the iframe gains focus. Never the interactive surface.
  • CWS (v5 #036) — light reuse: The leftmost compact-pane tab is "Sites" with a live status dot and counter (3 saved); clicking it is the canonical door to the Library inside the running session. Adapted from the corner-widget pattern, kept inside the pane to stay within "one secondary surface".

The launcher in Frame A is not a separate "modal" or "drawer" — it is literally the secondary pane's content. After boot, the pane's content swaps to the 6-tab Site Manager; the pane itself never goes away.
