v7 / 029 — Three Glass Surfaces · Strict Tiers

The only chrome ever visible is three glass surfaces — a top capsule, a bottom dock, and a right pill. Each lives at its own quantified blur tier (8 / 18 / 36 px). The iframe is the only solid surface; everything else is one of those three or hidden. Depth is a strict visual hierarchy, never a click target. Composes GLS (glassmorphism) + 3LD (three-layer depth as visual style only) with DEV (the slim top capsule) and CWS (the right pill is the persistent live-status Sites door).
