# Review of v7 #028 — Ventura Sidebar · Smart Resize

**Direction:** "Ventura-style left sidebar of 5 categories is the only chrome. Smart-resize governs the sidebar↔iframe seam: 6 snap detents (56/280/432/720/1008/1440), momentum gauge etched into the seam, whichever side you interact with grows."

**Note:** This design has **no README.md**. Review is based on `index.html` and `meta.json` only.

**Composed primitives (claimed → verified):**
- **VEN (Ventura sidebar)** — claimed → **verified**. The `.sidebar` is a tinted left column with traffic lights at the title bar, `.wpmark` + name/meta stack, `.savepill`, search field, 5 categories with tinted gradient `.glyph` icons (live/sites/settings/blueprint/logs colour tokens declared), keyboard hints (`⌘K`, `⌘1–5`). Each category collapses Site Manager tabs into the sidebar — explicitly "5 categories = 5 Site Manager tabs."
- **SR (Smart Resize)** — claimed → **verified, and rendered with high fidelity**. Six snap detents are *actually drawn* as `.snap-rail` dots (56/280/432/720/1008/1440) at every panel's seam. The `.gauge` is etched into the seam with a 3-bar momentum graphic + ratio readout (e.g., `280 / 1160`) + a `→ iframe` direction arrow that flips to `← sidebar` in Panel B when the sidebar is the grower. `⌘[ ⌘]` keyboard hint visible. Snap-rail's `on` dot shifts position per panel — Panel A's active dot is at 280, Panel B at 432, Panel D at 720, Panel F at 720, Panel C at 56. This is the most fully-rendered SR primitive in v7 so far.
- **DEV (no top bar — replaced by an inline iframe URL strip)** — claimed implicitly. There is no global top bar. The iframe pane has a small `.frame-url` strip (refresh + scheme + path + more) at the top of the iframe only. Outside the iframe pane there's no chrome — the title bar uses macOS traffic lights + a thin `titlebar-label`, no URL.
- **Apply+Reset destructive escalation** — auto-snapshot row + JSON diff block + typed-phrase confirm (`reset playground`) in Panel E. Brief requirement met explicitly.
- **MOB** — **CSS-only, never rendered**. `.mobile`, `.mobile-inner`, `.mobile-top`, `.mobile-sheet`, `.mobile-tabbar`, `.mobile-row` classes all exist (lines 1108-1240) but `grep "class=\"mobile"` returns 0 matches in markup. Mobile is asserted by the stylesheet, not demonstrated.

**Affordance count at idle (Panel A):** sidebar with 5 categories (counted as 1 by Apple-dock convention) + search + save-pill + 3 actions in pane-head (lock/home/more) + 3 rows in the action group-card (address/reload/fullscreen kbd) + sidebar-foot Fullscreen button + iframe-url strip (refresh + scheme + path + more) = ~ **11 strict, 7 grouped**. README's claimed count cannot be checked (no README). Strict count is *over* the 8-affordance budget; grouped is under.

**Scores (out of 10):**
- Composition: 8 — VEN + SR genuinely compose. The seam between sidebar and iframe *is* both the Ventura divider AND the smart-resize control surface, which is a single-purpose double-use. Five categories = five Site Manager tabs is named and rendered (each category panel has a header that matches the Site Manager tab name).
- Banned patterns avoided: 9 — no two-row top bar (no top bar at all); no depth-as-control (no blur layers as control); no terminal echo. The iframe-URL strip inside the iframe pane is one-row only.
- Restraint: 6 — strict count is ~ 11 affordances, soft count ~ 7. Without a README to declare the convention, this is over budget on the strict reading. The Live-preview category's sidebar pane shows an "address / reload / fullscreen" *info card* on top of an actual address row in the iframe — that's two address surfaces.
- Iframe primacy: 7 — iframe pane occupies 1160/1440 (80%) at rest and 1384/1440 (96%) when the sidebar collapses to 56 px. Fullscreen has *four* anchors: a `fs-btn` in the sidebar-foot, a `fs-corner` on the iframe (Panel C), an `Open fullscreen` row in the Live pane's action card (Panel A), and the `F` keyboard shortcut. That's a lot of fullscreen doors for "one door per feature."
- One-door / one-secondary: 6 — Fullscreen has 4 surfaces (sidebar-foot button + fs-corner + Live-pane row + F key). Save has 2 (sidebar-head savepill + inline `Save` button inside the savepill). Settings has *two depths* (sidebar category "Settings" + Sites pane → "Settings" tab? — not fully visible). The pane swap is the single secondary surface (good), but the affordance multiplication for fullscreen is the design's weakest area.
- Direction adherence: 9 — the direction's exact 6 detents (56/280/432/720/1008/1440) are rendered as snap-rail dots, the momentum gauge shows which side is growing, and Panel C demonstrates the 56-px rail collapse. "Five categories also serve as Site Manager tabs" is rendered.
- Visual fidelity: 9 — Inter substitute for SF Pro, Lucide icons (loaded via CDN), real coffee-shop WP content (Madrona Coffee, Verona Dark, Costa Rica Tarrazú), real Apply+Reset diff with `preferredVersions` and `features.networking` JSON keys, full 43-blueprint library with 10 categories + 18 cards visible, real PHP log lines hinted in Panel F.
- Synthesis quality: 8 — VEN and SR cohabit the seam: the seam is the Ventura divider AND the SR control. The gauge sits at the seam with the snap-rail beneath it. This is the cleanest VEN+SR composition in v7 to date. The synthesis is somewhat undermined by the absence of mobile and the absence of a README explaining the choice.

## What works

- The momentum gauge etched into the seam with a 3-bar bouncing graphic + ratio readout + direction arrow is the most legible smart-resize gauge of any v7 design. The snap-rail dots beneath it show all 6 detents visually, with the current one filled — the user can see *where they can go* before they drag.
- The direction arrow flips per state: Panel A shows `→ iframe` (iframe will grow if you click the iframe), Panel B shows `← sidebar` (sidebar just grew). This makes the SR primitive's "whichever you interact with grows" rule legible in a still image — most SR designs need a video to communicate this.
- Five categories = five Site Manager tabs is correctly fused: there is no separate "Site Manager" surface; the sidebar IS the Site Manager. Each category's `pane-body` swaps content. This is the cleanest one-door-per-feature execution in a Ventura-style design.
- Apply+Reset modal is correctly escalated: auto-snapshot with named timestamp (`unsaved-2026-05-21T14-03-12.zip · 142 MB`), real JSON diff with `preferredVersions.wp/php` and `features.networking` changes, typed-phrase confirm (`reset playground`) with a match indicator. Three guards layered.
- Lucide icons (loaded via CDN) give the design real iconography — the play / grid-2x2 / sliders-horizontal / file-code-2 / terminal-square category glyphs are the canonical icon set, not hand-drawn approximations.
- Panel C explicitly demonstrates the 56-px rail collapse: when the user clicks the iframe, the sidebar's labels disappear, only the 5 tinted glyphs remain, and the iframe grows to ~ 1384 px. The rail still shows the category accent colours.
- Apply+Reset uses an actual JSON diff with context lines (` ` markers) and del/add markers (`-`/`+`) — not just a "WordPress will change" sentence.
- Panel H's library overlay renders 18 cards (well above the 16 minimum) with realistic blueprint names from the actual catalog.

## What's weak or missing

- **No README.md.** The brief mandates a README with thesis + primitives + remix + trade-offs + self-critique. This design has none, so the "composition is the v7 thesis" requirement (principle 6) can't be verified at the prose level — only by reading the HTML. The meta.json `summary` is one paragraph and substitutes thinly.
- **No mobile frame.** The stylesheet declares `.mobile / .mobile-top / .mobile-sheet / .mobile-tabbar` (lines 1108-1240) but `grep 'class="mobile' index.html` returns 0 matches. The brief requires "Both 1440×900 desktop and 390×844 mobile rendered." Hard requirement not met.
- **Fullscreen has four affordances.** Sidebar-foot Fullscreen button, fs-corner on the iframe (Panel C), an "Open fullscreen" row inside the Live pane's info card (Panel A), and the `F` keyboard shortcut. That's a lot of doors for a single verb. The Live pane's action card also lists an `Address /` row alongside the real address row inside the iframe URL strip — two address surfaces.
- **The 56-px rail collapse (Panel C) shows the iframe URL strip *and* the rail's tinted glyphs simultaneously**, but the iframe URL strip is *the only place* the iframe URL can be read. When the rail is at 56 px, there's no way to type a new URL — that has to come from elsewhere, which isn't explained.
- **The "Live preview" category in the sidebar pane shows a redundant info card** with three rows (Address / Reload iframe / Open fullscreen) that duplicate what the iframe URL strip already shows. This is borderline dual-channel — the pane echoes the iframe's chrome.
- **6 snap detents is itself a lot** — the v5 #023 spec called for 35/65, 50/50, 60/40, 75/25 (4 detents). This design's 6 detents include `1440/0` (sidebar fills the entire window with no iframe) and `56/1384` (sidebar collapsed to a rail). The 0-iframe detent is interesting but means "the iframe completely disappears" — which violates iframe primacy.
- **Without a README**, the affordance count is unstated and the "VEN + SR" composition claim has no prose justification.

## How to push harder

- Write the README. The composition is genuinely strong (VEN seam = SR control) and deserves a thesis paragraph. Without one, the design ships its argument purely through the HTML.
- Render mobile. The stylesheet is half-written; finish it. At 390 × 844 the 6 detents collapse to 2 (peek / full) and the sidebar becomes a bottom sheet — that's a clean MOB story.
- Resolve the four-fullscreen-doors situation. Pick one anchor (likely the sidebar-foot button + `F` key) and remove the others. The action card row "Open fullscreen" is the most obvious one to drop — it duplicates the foot button.
- Remove the Live-preview pane's info card (Address / Reload / Fullscreen). The iframe URL strip already shows the address and the reload, and the foot button shows fullscreen. The info card is pure restatement.
- Reconsider the `1440 / 0` detent. If the iframe disappears entirely, what's left? A category-pane that fills the window? Then the design is no longer a smart-resize *between two panes* but a "iframe-or-nothing" toggle. Either justify or drop this detent.
- The `frame-url` strip inside the iframe pane (refresh + scheme + path + more) is duplicating the address row in the Live-preview info card. Pick one location for the URL.

## Lesson for the next v7 designer

Writing the README is not optional — your composition thesis must be defensible in prose, because a critic counting affordances or doors without your stated convention will count them strictly, and you'll bust your budget.
