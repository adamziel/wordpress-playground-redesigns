# V8 Directions

All directions share the same structural backbone from V7 #010: quiet rail, one pane, depth tiers, persistent Sites widget, live iframe, mobile bottom rail. The difference is the design system and the interaction emphasis.

1. **Precision Editorial** — Crisp black/white editorial interface, sharp typography, thin rules, sparse controls, one loud pane like a publication editing desk.
2. **Warm Paper Studio** — Tactile paper, ink, folders, ledger-like reset flow, soft depth from shadows and stacked sheets rather than glass.
3. **Mica Operating System** — Windows/Mica restraint, translucent but quiet, settings-app ergonomics, calm rounded controls, wallpaper-sampled tint.
4. **Material Elevation** — Material 3 surfaces, explicit elevation tokens, state layers, compact nav rail, depth as shadow/elevation not blur.
5. **Developer Console Pro** — DevTools-like density, monospace metadata, inspector pane, structured logs, but no terminal echo duplication.
6. **Premium Product Glass** — High-end SaaS polish, restrained glass, low chrome, strong focus/fullscreen route, Sites widget as jewel-like status object.
7. **Bento Operations** — Modular bento panels inside one pane, dashboard-like grouping without making the home screen a dashboard.
8. **Swiss Corporate** — Enterprise clarity, forms/tables, strong IA labels, conservative colors, accessibility-first contrast and spacing.
9. **Mobile-First Bottom Rail** — 390px mobile is canonical; desktop is a wider phone. Bottom rail, bottom sheet, horizontal Sites status capsule.
10. **Spatial Calm Depth** — Subtle spatial memory and parallax-like depth, but no spatial canvas navigation. Depth tiers show focus distance around the same rail/pane structure.
