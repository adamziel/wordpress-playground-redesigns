# v11 Directions — Ten Dark Skins of the Quiet Rail

Read `BRIEF.md` first. All ten designs preserve the locked v7 #010 interaction model. They are dark-theme only and respect the taste constraints in §"Hard taste constraints" of the brief: no purple, no monospace outside the code editor, no typical fonts, multiple distinct rail buttons à la Forest Botanical, no icon / color proliferation, no borders inside borders, no per-button secondary text, simplicity over decoration.

Synthesized from two independent brainstorms. Each direction below is a starting brief — the designer must push it further and answer the focus test in the README.

---

### 001 — Atelier Steel
- **Idiom**: a quiet architect's night studio. Cool charcoal, single bone foil, generous negative space, one typeface.
- **Backgrounds**: rail `#0E1013` · pane `#14171B` · iframe area `#080A0D`.
- **Text**: ink `#E6E4DC`, secondary `#737880`.
- **Accent**: bone-warm `#D7CDB6` — only on the active dock's left indicator and the saving pill text. **No chromatic accent at all.**
- **Typefaces**: **Geist** (Google Fonts, two weights — 500 headline, 400 body). One face does everything.
- **Rail buttons**: five two-letter monograms in Geist medium 14 px (`Si`, `SM`, `Bp`, `Db`, `Lg`). Inactive at secondary; active lifts to ink-primary and gains a 2 px bone vertical bar at its left edge. Identity mark above is a tiny `P` in the same set.
- **Borders**: none. Surfaces separate by tone only. Pane head: a single 1 px `#1F2227` rule beneath the title.
- **Corner widget**: a typeset stack (site name + state) — no frame, no border.
- **Pane**: 5-dock; each opens its own page.
- **Focus test**: zero color, one typeface — the iframe is the only chromatic surface on screen.

### 002 — Charcoal Foundry
- **Idiom**: a heavy iron foundry at dusk — matte black metal lit by a dim warm bulb.
- **Backgrounds**: rail `#0E0E0E` · pane `#141413` · iframe area `#0A0A0A`.
- **Text**: ink `#E9E3D2`, secondary `#7A7368`.
- **Accent**: amber `#E08A2C` — saving pill + 2 px left bar on the active rail dock. **Nowhere else.**
- **Typefaces**: **Mona Sans** (Google Fonts, Wide cut for the headline at 500–600; Regular for body at 400). Distinctive industrial voice, not Inter.
- **Rail buttons**: five hand-set 2-letter ligatures in Mona Sans small caps (`Si` `Mn` `Bp` `Db` `Lg`), ink-on-charcoal, no boxes. Active lifts to ink and gains amber bar.
- **Borders**: a single 1 px `#222` rule between rail and pane. Cards inside the pane have no border — only a 1 px hairline under section heads.
- **Corner widget**: a soft-edged charcoal lozenge — site name in Mona Sans, small ember-amber dot.
- **Pane**: 5-dock.
- **Focus test**: the entire chrome reads as one matte slab of metal; nothing competes with the iframe.

### 003 — Observatory Readout
- **Idiom**: an astronomical control desk at 2 a.m. — flat dark, faint horizon rule, single warm-amber star.
- **Backgrounds**: rail `#0B0E12` · pane `#10141A` · iframe area `#070A0E`.
- **Text**: ink `#E8E2D2`, secondary `#7A8290`.
- **Accent**: warm amber `#E5A24A` — active rail dock's 2 px left bar + saving pill dot only.
- **Typefaces**: **Fraunces** (Google Fonts, low-contrast soft cut, 500 for headline / pane titles) + **Public Sans** (400/500, body and forms). Fraunces for the antique-instrument feel; Public Sans keeps form labels honest.
- **Rail buttons**: five **celestial marks**, each genuinely distinct silhouette — Sites = ringed dot, Site Manager = an `SM` italic Fraunces ligature, Blueprint = a transit-line dash with a tick, Database = three stacked horizon bars, Logs = a Morse triplet. All in ink-secondary 22 px; active becomes ink and the amber bar appears at left.
- **Borders**: pane has one outer 1 px `#1B2028` border. Internal sections separated by a single 1 px bottom rule, never a card-in-card.
- **Corner widget**: a horizontal "magnitude bar" — site name in Fraunces italic, tiny amber dot, one stat line.
- **Pane**: 5-dock.
- **Focus test**: the screen reads like one calm dial array — nothing competes with the iframe.

### 004 — Onyx Museum
- **Idiom**: a museum-after-hours gallery — black walls, a single warm picture light on the iframe.
- **Backgrounds**: rail `#0C0C0D` · pane `#121214` · iframe area `#08080A`.
- **Text**: ink `#E8E5DC`, secondary `#787268`.
- **Accent**: bone-cream `#D9CFAA` — active dock and saving pill only. Reads as warm light, not color.
- **Typefaces**: **Spectral** (Google Fonts, 500 for pane titles and rail numerals) + **Plus Jakarta Sans** (400/500, body). Museum label + curatorial body.
- **Rail buttons**: five wall-label numerals `I`, `II`, `III`, `IV`, `V` in Spectral 22 px at ink-secondary; active turns to bone-cream. Identity mark is a small bone-cream `P` in matching style.
- **Borders**: a single hairline `#1E1E1F` between rail and pane. Cards are borderless; section heads use a typographic eyebrow only.
- **Corner widget**: a wall-label rectangle — bone-cream ink on near-black, one line of state, no border.
- **Pane**: 5-dock (each tool is "a room").
- **Focus test**: gallery silence — only one object is lit.

### 005 — Brutalist Editorial (Le Monde Diplomatique After Dark)
- **Idiom**: a serious newspaper turned night-mode — single ruled column logic, ink-secondary headlines.
- **Backgrounds**: rail `#101112` · pane `#161718` · iframe area `#0B0B0C`.
- **Text**: ink `#EDEAE2`, secondary `#7E7B72`.
- **Accent**: red-ochre `#B14A39` — only on the saving pill text. The active dock simply goes ink-primary (no chromatic accent on rail at all).
- **Typefaces**: **Spectral** (display, 500–600) for the masthead / pane titles + **Public Sans** (400, body and forms). Spectral as the front-page mast; Public Sans as the agate.
- **Rail buttons**: vertical typeset column — each dock is its full label set in Spectral 12 px italic (`Sites`, `Manager`, `Blueprint`, `Database`, `Logs`). Inactive at ink-secondary; active bolds to ink-primary with a 2 px ink-primary left bar. "Distinct buttons" come from each word having a uniquely-shaped silhouette.
- **Borders**: single 1 px outer pane border `#202023`. Internal: one rule at the head, no others.
- **Corner widget**: a tiny dateline — italic site name + dot.
- **Pane**: 5-dock — each tool is its own article.
- **Focus test**: a paper with no graphics teaches the eye to read structure.

### 006 — Inked Schema (Technical Journal)
- **Idiom**: an 1890s engineering journal printed in dark mode — diagrammatic, captioned, zero decoration.
- **Backgrounds**: rail `#0C0E10` · pane `#12151A` · iframe area `#070A0E`.
- **Text**: ink `#E4E1D7`, secondary `#7A7B72`.
- **Accent**: ink-blue `#5C8AA6` — saving pill + active dock left bar.
- **Typefaces**: **Crimson Pro** (Google Fonts, 500 display) for the journal mast and pane titles + **Public Sans** (400/500 for body and schedules). Crimson Pro has the technical-journal weight; Public Sans matches its column rhythm.
- **Rail buttons**: each dock is a **"figure" caption** — numbered Crimson Pro `Fig. 1` through `Fig. 5` set 10 px italic, with a single hand-drawn schema mark beneath (triangle, gear-tooth notch, cross-section ladder, cylinder stack, scribble line) at 18 px ink-secondary. Each mark genuinely unique. Active: caption turns ink-primary; mark gains a thin ink-blue underline.
- **Borders**: 1 px outer pane border `#1A1D22`. Internal blocks have no borders. Inside, a single 1 px bottom rule per section header.
- **Corner widget**: a caption block — `Fig. — Coffee Shop` italic, status secondary below, no frame.
- **Pane**: 5-dock — each figure has its own page.
- **Focus test**: numbered figures + single muted blue = eye scans and places itself instantly.

### 007 — Tokaido Lacquer
- **Idiom**: lacquered black with one rusted vermilion seal — the restraint of a Japanese product manual.
- **Backgrounds**: rail `#0A0B0C` · pane `#13151A` · iframe area `#06070A`.
- **Text**: ink `#E9E4D9`, secondary `#7B7468`.
- **Accent**: vermilion `#C24A2C` — active dock's 4×4 square seal mark + saved-state pill.
- **Typefaces**: **Newsreader** italic (Google Fonts, 500) for the identity and pane titles + **Plus Jakarta Sans** (400/500, body and forms). Newsreader's calligraphic tilt is the seal-script echo.
- **Rail buttons**: a column of five **woodblock-style glyphs** hand-built inline as SVG — Sites = horizon line over water, Site Manager = a key, Blueprint = a folded paper crease, Database = a stacked-stone cairn, Logs = a single brush stroke. Each unique silhouette; no surrounding shape. Active gets the vermilion seal square beside it.
- **Borders**: none. Sections separated by a 1 px `#1F1B17` rule with a 4 px vermilion square at the leading end, used once per pane header.
- **Corner widget**: a small unframed two-line stamp — site name in Newsreader italic, status word below in Plus Jakarta small caps.
- **Pane**: 5-dock.
- **Focus test**: the only red on the screen is wherever your attention should be; nothing else even has a frame.

### 008 — Monastery Scriptorium (Nightwatch)
- **Idiom**: a hand-illuminated manuscript at candlelight — dark vellum, single gilt initial per item.
- **Backgrounds**: rail `#0E0C09` · pane `#15120D` · iframe area `#0A0805`.
- **Text**: ink `#E6DCC2`, secondary `#7C7058`.
- **Accent**: gilt `#B3893D` — active dock's hand-drawn initial fill + save-state pill.
- **Typefaces**: **Cormorant Garamond** (Google Fonts, 500 display, used ONLY on rail initials and pane titles) + **Atkinson Hyperlegible** (400/500, all body and forms — accessibility because dark BGs wreck legibility unless the body face is engineered for it). Mono in code editor only.
- **Rail buttons**: five illuminated capitals — **S**, **M**, **B**, **D**, **L** — set 28 px Cormorant Garamond ink-secondary, each with a single hand-drawn quill flourish in SVG curling around the active one. Active: initial fills gilt; flourish appears in ink-secondary.
- **Borders**: a single 1 px `#2A2418` ruling line at the head of each pane. No card outlines.
- **Corner widget**: a small inked footer mark — site name italic, dot status.
- **Pane**: 5-dock — each tool is its own chapter page, headed by its illuminated initial.
- **Focus test**: the rail looks like a page index, not controls; cognitive load drops to "pick a chapter".

### 009 — Graphite Engineer
- **Idiom**: an engineering report binder — black-graphite, single drafting-red accent.
- **Backgrounds**: rail `#111315` · pane `#16191B` · iframe area `#0B0D0F`.
- **Text**: ink `#E2E4E6`, secondary `#7C8389`.
- **Accent**: drafting red `#B84A39` — saving pill + 2 px left active bar.
- **Typefaces**: **Space Grotesk** (Google Fonts, 500–600 headline) + **Public Sans** (400/500 body). Technical voice without becoming Inter. Space Grotesk numerals are particularly distinctive.
- **Rail buttons**: five stamped numerals `01` `02` `03` `04` `05` in Space Grotesk 16 px, ink-secondary; active turns to ink-primary and gains the drafting-red bar. Identity mark is a `P` set in the same numeral style at the top.
- **Borders**: a single 1 px rule between rail and pane. Otherwise no borders.
- **Corner widget**: a small report-tab with site name and an `01:` prefix.
- **Pane**: 5-dock — each pane has a numbered section head (`03 · Blueprint`) and no further chrome.
- **Focus test**: numbers are silent; only their position changes when active. The rail is recognizable but never decorative.

### 010 — Subterranean Telecom
- **Idiom**: a Cold-War switching room — graphite, muted teal, all light comes from inside the panel.
- **Backgrounds**: rail `#0C0F0E` · pane `#11161A` · iframe area `#070A0B`.
- **Text**: ink `#D8E2DA`, secondary `#6A7872`.
- **Accent**: muted signal teal `#3E8A78` (NOT phosphor — muted) — active dock 2 px left bar, nothing else.
- **Typefaces**: **Recursive Sans** (Google Fonts, Linear style, 400 body / 600 headline). One face flexed by axis. Crucially: Recursive Linear is **proportional**, not monospace — pair labels and paths use it. Mono in code editor only.
- **Rail buttons**: five hand-cut **"patch labels"** — each a Recursive uppercase pair (`SI`, `SM`, `BP`, `DB`, `LG`) with a 2-character serial code typeset to the right at half size in a darker tone (e.g. `SI/01`, `SM/02`, …, `LG/05`). Reads as a switch-panel engraving, not an icon. Active brightens to ink-primary and the teal bar appears at left.
- **Borders**: a single 1 px outer pane border `#1A2120`. Internal: a single 1 px bottom rule per section. Never a nested box.
- **Corner widget**: a horizontal engraved bar — `RUNNING · WP 6.7 · 14.2 MB` set with bullets, no box, just a 1 px underline.
- **Pane**: 5-dock — instruments are individually labeled.
- **Focus test**: monolithic palette + engraved labeling means nothing is decorative; eye lands on the iframe.

## What every designer must check before shipping

A red-pencil pass against the v11 taste constraints. If you find **any** of the following in your own design, fix before submitting:

- [ ] Background tones outside `#06–#1A`.
- [ ] A purple / plum / violet / magenta-leaning hex anywhere.
- [ ] Monospace font used anywhere except inside a `.code-editor` block.
- [ ] Inter, Roboto, Helvetica, Arial, ui-sans-serif, or JetBrains Mono referenced in @font-face / font-family for chrome.
- [ ] More than one accent hue in the design (state colors not counted, but they should be a single muted lift, not a separate hue).
- [ ] The rail has any element other than: identity mark + 5 dock buttons.
- [ ] A border appears inside another border in any card.
- [ ] Any dock button has a secondary line of text beneath it.
- [ ] A "tip", "help", "shortcut" floater anywhere in the chrome.
- [ ] The screen has so much going on that a person cannot point at the rail / pane / capsule / iframe in five seconds.

Your README must answer: **"Why is the iframe the loudest object on the screen?"** in one sentence.
