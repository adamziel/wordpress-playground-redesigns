Monastery Scriptorium · v11 design 008. Single accent (gilt #B3893D), used only twice — active-letter fill + Saved-state pill. Cormorant Garamond strictly restricted to the rail's five capitals + pane titles; everywhere else, including all log lines, settings labels, sub-text and path labels, is Atkinson Hyperlegible because that face is engineered for legibility on dark backgrounds. Mono is permitted only inside the file-editor and blueprint-json blocks. Backgrounds in #06–#1A. No purple. The iframe is the loudest object because the rail looks like a page index, not a control deck — your eye lands on the only chromatic, fully-saturated surface on the screen.
